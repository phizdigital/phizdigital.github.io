Lake District September Zeitgeist

21 September 2017

Digital content containing rich media is good for engagement, SEO and the user experience.

A zeitgeist for September 2017 in the Lake District. As the planet's most recently crowned UNESCO world heritage site, this autumn and winter should prove to be particularly interesting and this post, written for the Lakelovers blog attempts to capture all of the anticipated excitement.

Why High Quality Content Matters

Exceptional copywriting and some amazing images make for an interesting and informative piece of work. Engagement and the rewards that accompany it, is best achieved by featuring content that has been well thought through. In other words, expertly produced content, that sits in line with the interests and values of both the publisher and the intended audience.

While link building and SEO are important, featuring poorly written content that has been produced cheaply to serve them will not impress your clientele.

This blog post reflects the ethos of the company and their mission to provide their customers with a comprehensive service, useful information and an amazing holiday experience.
