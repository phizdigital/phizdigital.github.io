Blog Post: Bags & Rifles

25 May 2017

This blog post covers the World Gunmakers Evening held in London, May 18 2017. The event attracts the elite from the world of field sports gun manufacturing.

The blog was written for Chapman Bags who's heritage is in the production of accessories for the field sports niche.

Handcrafted from their Cumbrian base and sold globally, this is a very high calibre brand and a fantastic business.

Copywriting for a high-end luggage brand who include model Marina Gibson, Michelin starred chef Kevin Tickle and travel journalist Tom Barber as their ambassadors is a real blast and we are aiming to do more work for Chapman Bags very soon.
