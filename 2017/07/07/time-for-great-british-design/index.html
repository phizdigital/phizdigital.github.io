Time for Great British Design

07 July 2017

This is a blog post for the Chapman Bags Journal, featuring Farer a luxury watch brand that are really making some waves at the moment.

Time for a New Bag

Our brief from Chapman is to write a series of posts that will drive traffic to their website, help with conversions and expose the brand to a wider luxury demographic. Each post is keyword optimised and is delivered with images and advice on how to best promote it on the companies social channels.

As the project moves forward, we will be writing guest blog posts for Chapman in order to get back links from like minded companies who are champions of British design and manufacturing.

Collaboration, mutual support and respect are paramount with this approach to digital marketing.

Many thanks Farer who have been welcoming, generous and kind.
