Phiz Digital: Just a Click Away

09 March 2017

Phiz Digital Launch New Website

Manchester UK, March 2017 – Phiz Digital a company specialising in the production of digital content for businesses, have launched a website to promote their services.

The website is elegant, it features photographs taken by the company directors four year old son, has a monochrome theme and cost absolutely nothing to build.

“We’ve been setting the foundation stones of the company for some time now and the site will play a pivotal role in its development moving forward. Building the site has been a real family affair, my son Grayson took the photographs used on the site, I produced the copy and my brother-in-law Chris was responsible for the coding wizardry” Paul Whittland, Director.

The site itself is very simple, featuring just five pages it feels like a website/blog hybrid, there’s an almost ghostly art-house feel to it and there are no gimmicks or pop ups.

Looks aside, the site contains some interesting information, the Clients and Friends page lists the companies that Phiz are currently working with. Luxury brands are who Phiz Digital specialise in helping - Chapman make luxury bags and are champions of the made in Briton ethos, while VIP is one of the biggest suppliers of premium vaping products and e-liquid in the UK and overseas.

“We’re really happy with the clients we are working with and for the moment we are at capacity in terms of workload which is great. We will soon be making provision for more clients, but our primary objective is to make sure our current clients have everything they need” Louise Whittland, Director.

Phiz Digital’s business plan for the next 12 months involves winning more clients and taking on more staff to accommodate this expansion.

“The site really encapsulates the company and its ethos, it’s simple, honest and great to get involved with.” Louise Whittland.
