Infographic: The Art of Sub Ohming

23 March 2017

Another fantastic infographic piece for a high ranking website.

This version of the piece is for an A3 POS asset that will be used in VIP retail outlets across the UK.

Many people who but sub ohm vaping devices happily use them without knowing anything about how they work or the emerging subculture that have embraced them. The purpose of this infographic is to inform and to raise awareness of both those things.

More superb graphics from Rik Cheetham - using just 2 colours gives the piece drama, cool aesthetics and clarity.
