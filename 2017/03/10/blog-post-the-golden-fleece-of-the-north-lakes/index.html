Blog Post: The Golden Fleece of the North

10 March 2017

This was a copywriting brief to deliver an uplifting post for the Lakelovers Blog following the devastation of Storm Desmond and the floods that followed.

Plus, the client had not long since opened their Northern office in Keswick, which was looking for some positive PR also.

During that week, from the 3rd-8th December 2016, Lakelovers, the longest established self-catering cottage holiday agency in the Lake District, were keen to remind folk of the beauty of Ullswater and the North Lake District.

As we are Lovers of the Lakes ourselves, we were very happy to help!

We only need to look at photography of the region to be reminded of why famous artists like Beatrix Potter, William Wordsworth and Wainright were mesmerised by the unrivalled beauty and poetic charm of this quiet part of the the English Lakes.

Personally, knowing the warmth of the North Lake's community and all that this area has to offer, it was a pleasure to write this blog post.

Why The North Lakes Has It All

Paul Whittland
