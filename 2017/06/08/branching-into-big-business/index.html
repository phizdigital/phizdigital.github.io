Branching into Big Business

08 June 2017

VIP are currently our main client and they have recently become a subsidiary of British American Tobacco (BAT). This means we are now working, indirectly, for a multinational company.

Our remit for VIP is basically to be their written voice and so all of their copywriting; web content, product descriptions etc are written by us - oh and we write and manage their blog too.

And, although we won't be working for BAT directly, we will be liaising with some of their staff and will need to adhere to the protocols of big business.

We see this as a fantastic opportunity to be involved in digital marketing on a completely new level, one that is challenging, inspiring and that stretches our creative and technical skills to the limit.

Love Phiz Digital X
